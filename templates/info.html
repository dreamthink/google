If you were looking for a Google search, you won't find it here.

According to the Nielsen's Total Audience Report, Americans aged 18 and older spend more than 11 hours a day watching TV, listening to the radio, or using smartphones and other electronic devices. It may be hard to believe, but we once survived just fine without the Internet, social media, and all of our other gadgets that we own today.

So why don't you unplug for a day and go "low-tech"? Your brain will thank you.
